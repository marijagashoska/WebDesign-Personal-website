Trdlo

Trdlo is a spit cake that is made from dough that is wrapped around a stick, then baked and topped with sugar and walnut mix. You can combine trdlo with different creams and topings- similar to Milky Pancakes.
The crunchy taste of trdlo is a must try!

Pancakes in Ohrid

During summer time I enjoy taking long walks along the coast of Ohrid Lake. How do I make these walks more enjoyable for myself?
Well I always eat something sweet, usually pancakes. Eurokrem, banana and plazma is the most common combination, but it never gets old.
The only thing that can make the things even better, is a good company!

Milky Pancakes

Look no further because the sweet, creamy, delicious Milky Pancakes are just a couple of minutes away from Skopje city center.
Once you taste these pancakes, your perception for desserts will never be the same again.
There are different flavours of creams and toppings you can apply, and besides the sweet pancakes, you can also try a salty combination.
